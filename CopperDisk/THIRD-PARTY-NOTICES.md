# Third-Party Notices

## xDMS

CopperDisk's DMS decoder follows the file-format validation and decrunch
behavior of xDMS, a public-domain DMS unpacker by Andre Rodrigues de la Rocha.
The implementation is maintained in C# inside CopperDisk and does not invoke or
ship the xDMS binary.

- Project: https://github.com/timofonic-retro/xdms
- Status: Public domain
